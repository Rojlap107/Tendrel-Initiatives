TenDrel Pathways

Where Mind and Media Meet Moral Futures

At the heart of the TenDrel Initiative lies a living framework that transforms vision into action—where communication becomes a force for consciousness, connection, and collective care. Guided by the Tibetan principle of tendrel (interdependence and auspicious connection), these pathways shape TenDrel's mission to create a more conscious, equitable, and sustainable planet.

01

Building Digital Presence & Media Outreach

Building a dynamic online platform that amplifies stories of interconnection through reflections, short videos, and creative content grounded in moral imagination.

Image 01

02

Creating Blogs & Podcasts: Interconnected Futures

Creating multimedia spaces that merge scholarship, lived experience, and storytelling to illuminate the intersections of mind, media, and planetary well-being.

Image 02

03

Hosting Quarterly Dialogues

Hosting reflective conversations that bring together thinkers, activists, and artists to explore eco-ethical consciousness and communication, gender equity, social change, and moral futures.

Image 03

04

Producing Digital Storytelling Projects

Producing creative works—short films, music, and multimedia collaborations—that bridge mind, media, and morality through art and expression.

05

Empowering Youth through the TenDrel Fellowship

Empowering emerging global citizens to design and lead climate storytelling and values-based leadership projects, mentored by TenDrel's founder and collaborators.

Image 05

06

Engaging Communities through Local Initiatives

Engaging schools, colleges, and communities through storytelling workshops, dialogues, and media literacy programs that cultivate awareness and connection.

Image 06

07

Convening the Annual Forum: Mind, Media & Moral Futures

Convening interdisciplinary voices to explore how consciousness and digital media can shape more mindful and sustainable planetary action.

Image 07

08

Advancing Research & Knowledge Creation

Advancing interdisciplinary and humanistic social science research on communication, consciousness, and emerging media—exploring how human values, identity, culture, connection, and resilience drive social change, climate action, and planetary care.

Image 08

09

Strengthening Partnerships & Collaborative Networks

Strengthening alliances with universities, foundations, and global networks to expand TenDrel's impact and sustain its creative, research, and fellowship endeavors.

10

Sustaining Growth through Fundraising & Advancement Projects

Sustaining TenDrel's mission through innovative fundraising partnerships and advancement strategies that ensure its long-term vitality and reach.
