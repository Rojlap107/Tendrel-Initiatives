Rooted in the Tibetan concept of tendrel—interdependence and auspicious connection—the TenDrel Initiative envisions a world where mind, media, and moral imagination meet to shape a more equitable and sustainable planet.

Anchored in His Holiness the Dalai Lama's moral, intellectual, and spiritual leadership, TenDrel recognizes the interconnectedness of all life and promotes shared responsibility for planetary care through storytelling, reflection, and collaboration.

Ultimately, it reminds us that planetary care begins with a conscious mind.

TenDrel Pathway

The TenDrel Initiative transforms vision into action through four interconnected pathways that unite communication, consciousness, and collective care. From digital storytelling and youth fellowships to community engagement, research, and partnerships, these pathways embody TenDrel's living framework of interconnection. Together, they nurture a more conscious, equitable, and sustainable planet.
